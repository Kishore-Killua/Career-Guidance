Career Voyage

Finding the correct career path has become increasingly difficult in due to the multitude of options available and the rapidly evolving job market. The high cost and complexity of existing career guidance tools limit accessibility for many. Due to their complex and intricate interfaces and prohibitive costs these existing solutions fail to bridge the gap effectively. To address this challenge, this project proposes the development of a cutting-edge Career Guidance Application leveraging Artificial Intelligence technologies. This project proposes a intuitive web-based user interface to gather data from the user which is then analyzed to provide customized job recommendations for the user.
